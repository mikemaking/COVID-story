The importance of telling stories

Humans are narrative beings. We experience the word as a set of stories that we can then communicate to others. It is through sharing those stories that we learn as a community. Stories make up a network of information. Socializing our experiences is taking an active role in that network. One story is one pixel in the big picture that is humanity. And every pixel counts. Through mass media we might get a general glimpse of what's happening in the world. But through a single story we get to see what another human saw.
